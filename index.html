About the Smart Anganwadi Portal

The Smart Anganwadi Portal is a state-of-the-art digital welfare platform designed to modernize and streamline the operations of grassroots Anganwadi centers. Guided by the mission to combat child malnutrition and support maternal healthcare, we integrate tech-driven tools into the daily workflows of health workers.

By providing real-time data tracking, automated stock distribution, and direct digital reporting, we bridge the communication gap between local centers and central government administration, ensuring that resources reach the children and mothers who need them most.
